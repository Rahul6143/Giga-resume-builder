Yes, you can download your resume as a professional PDF file ready to send to employers.

You can start building without an account, but saving resumes for later requires signing up.

How long does it take to build a resume?

Most users finish within 10–15 minutes thanks to our pre-filled content suggestions.

Can I edit my resume after downloading?

You can re-upload or edit your saved version anytime from your dashboard.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes, your data is fully encrypted and never shared with third parties.

Can I build multiple resumes?

Yes! You can create and manage multiple resumes for different job roles or industries.

Do you offer AI suggestions or auto content?

Yes, our builder provides AI-generated phrases and section suggestions to enhance your resume.

What formats can I export in?

You can export in PDF, DOCX, or share a unique online link to your resume.
